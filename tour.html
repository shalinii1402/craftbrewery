Brewery Tours

Step Inside and See the Craft in Action

Experience the magic of Copper Creek firsthand. Join our immersive brewery tour and discover the artistry, dedication, and science that transform raw ingredients into our signature craft brews. From the hum of the tanks to the depth of the flavor, see where the soul of our beer is truly born.

The Experience

Join our expert guides on a 60-minute journey through our brewing facility. You'll learn about:

  • The history of Copper Creek
  • Our unique brewing process
  • Ingredients sourcing
  • Packaging and distribution

Includes: A flight of 4 tasters and a souvenir glass.

$25 per person

Highlights

The Brewhouse

Witness the start of the journey in our custom-built 30-barrel system. Smell the toasted grain and feel the heat of the kettle.

Fermentation Alley

Stand among the giants. Our massive fermentation vessels are where yeast works its magic under precise temperature control.

Cicerone-Led Tasting

Refine your palate. Learn to identify notes of citrus, pine, chocolate, and spice in our curated post-tour tasting flight.

Behind-the-Scenes Insights

Go where the public can't. Get an exclusive look at our quality control lab, raw ingredient storage, and our unique barrel-aging program. Understand the complexity of our water chemistry and yeast management that gives Copper Creek beers their distinct personality.

Our Philosophy

Guided Tasting Experiences

It's not just about drinking; it's about appreciation. Our tours conclude with a deep dive into sensory analysis. Learn how to evaluate appearance, aroma, mouthfeel, and finish under the guidance of our masters.

Discover the science of food and beer pairing during a guided session that will change the way you enjoy your next pint.
