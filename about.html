Our Story

From a garage home-brew setup to an award-winning craft brewery.

A Passion for Hops

Founded in 2012, Copper Creek started with a simple mission: to brew beer that we wanted to drink. Disappointed by the mass-market lagers, we set out to create something with character, depth, and soul.

What began as a weekend hobby quickly spiraled into an obsession. Today, we are proud to serve our community with a rotating tap list of over 20 unique brews, each with its own story.

Core Values

Community First

We believe breweries are the modern town square. We strive to create a welcoming space for all, fostering connections over every pint.

Sustainability

From water reclamation to spent grain donation for local livestock, we are committed to minimizing our footprint on the earth.

Excellence

We never cut corners. Quality ingredients, meticulous processes, and patience are the key to our award-winning brews.

Meet the Team

Head Brewer

James has been brewing since his college days, perfecting the art of the perfect IPA. With 15 years of experience, he leads our production with an uncompromising eye for quality.

Sarah Jenkins

Taproom Manager

A Legacy of Taste

  • 2012

    Copper Creek founded in a small garage in Craft City.

  • 2015

    Opened our first commercial taproom and 10bbl brewhouse.

  • 2019

    Won Gold at the National Brewers Cup for our "Midnight Stout".

  • 2024

    Expanding distribution to 3 neighboring states.
