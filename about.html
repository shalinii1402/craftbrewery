Our Story

From a garage home-brew setup to an award-winning craft brewery.

A Passion for Hops

Founded in 2012, Copper Creek started with a simple mission: to brew beer that we wanted to drink. Disappointed by the mass-market lagers, we set out to create something with character, depth, and soul.

What began as a weekend hobby quickly spiraled into an obsession. Today, we are proud to serve our community with a rotating tap list of over 20 unique brews, each with its own story.

Core Values

Community First

We believe breweries are the modern town square. We strive to create a welcoming space for all.

Sustainability

From water reclamation to spent grain donation for local livestock, we are committed to the earth.

Excellence

We never cut corners. Quality ingredients and patience are the key to our award-winning brews.
